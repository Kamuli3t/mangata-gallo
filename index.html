About Us

Mangata and Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in pieces for special occasions like engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Operating primarily online, Mangata & Gallo also maintains a small storefront in Austin, Texas, complete with an atelier for customers to browse and experience the craftsmanship firsthand. Known for its high-quality and classic designs, Mangata & Gallo's selection stands out in the jewelry market.

Our Founder

Mariana, the owner and lead designer of Mangata and Gallo, is an Austin local celebrated for her exquisite jewelry designs. After graduating from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana returned to her hometown to open her store. She has grown her business significantly through her online presence.

Our Team and Craftsmanship

From its inception, Mariana has overseen every aspect of Mangata and Gallo, from jewelry design and production to marketing and e-commerce. Recently, she expanded her team by hiring skilled artisans to help bring her designs to life and a young employee dedicated to managing the company's website and social media accounts.

Our Commitment

At Mangata and Gallo, we are committed to offering timeless, high-quality jewelry that makes every occasion special. Whether you're looking for the perfect engagement ring or a beautiful piece for an anniversary, we strive to provide exceptional service and craftsmanship to all our customers.
